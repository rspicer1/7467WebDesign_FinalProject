Welcome to Algebra Concepts & Connections!
Parents and Students, Welcome to Algebra Concepts & Connections. This website is designed for you to access frequently. Students will spend most time in the content section of the website. There is a page for guided notes and instructional videos that students can access when absent from school. There is also a page dedicated to Delta Math practice. I have embedded the Delta Math website on the Practice page; however, you may find it easier to open this website in a different tab if you are using a computer with a smaller screen. The same goes for the Desmos page.

We will be doing most Desmos activities within the classroom, and students will rarely be completing Desmos activities at home unless we have class project. Delta Math on the other hand is where most of our classroom practice and homework will be housed. Students, please make sure to log in with Google using your school email when accessing Delta Math and Desmos. Again, access this website regularly as there will be updates and more instructional videos added as we cover more material.

Parents, clicking "Home" above will take you to a tenative calendar pacing out the year. There is also a welcome letter with more information about this class.
